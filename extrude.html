"EXTRUDE" product branding

Our goal was to make a memorable modern design of chocolate products. It had to reflect quality and laconism of the brand.

I went through a lot of options, but in the end I settled on a minimal flat version, simple in production and attractive to the eye for buyers.

I developed identity, created mockups and a landing page, prepared several presentation videos.

The uniqueness of this design lies in the fact that it is easy to recreate and you can always add a wide variety of products that will match the design code.

Thanks for reading, and please rate the result.
